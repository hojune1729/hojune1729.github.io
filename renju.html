What is Renju?

Renju is a professional rule-set of the "five-in-a-row" game (gomoku), designed to reduce the first-move advantage by introducing special opening rules and forbidden moves for Black.

  • Renju (Wikipedia): https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Renju
  • m,n,k-game (Wikipedia): https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/M,n,k-game (Renju/Gomoku is a classic example of an m,n,k-game)
